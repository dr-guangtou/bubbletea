# Additional Reference of UCDs in Literature

- Blakeslee & Barber DeGraaff 2008
  - Title: Ultra-Compact Dwarf Candidates Near the Lensing Galaxy in Abell S0740
  - ADS: https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2008AJ....136.2295B/abstract
  - CDS: https://vizier.cds.unistra.fr/viz-bin/VizieR-3?-source=J/AJ/136/2295
  - Number of UCD candidates: 41
  - Notes: S0740 is at z=0.034, so this should be way out of Gaia's detection limit. It can serve as a negative test baseline.

- Gregg et al. 2009
  - Title: A Large Population of Ultra-Compact Dwarfs and Bright Intracluster Globulars in the Fornax Cluster
  - ADS: https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2009AJ....137..498G/abstract
  - CDS: https://vizier.cds.unistra.fr/viz-bin/VizieR-3?-source=J/AJ/137/498/table2
  - Number of UCD candidates: 60
  - Notes: should overlap with the newer NGC1399 sample, so need to perform internal cross-match to remove common objects. This serves as a double-check sample.

- Mieske et al. 2007
  - Title: A search for ultra-compact dwarf galaxies in the Centaurus galaxy cluster
  - ADS: https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2007A%26A...472..111M/abstract
  - CDS https://vizier.cds.unistra.fr/viz-bin/VizieR-3?-source=J/A%2bA/472/111/table1
  - Number of UCD candidates: 27
  - Notes: pay attention to overlap with existing sample; and here the 27 objects are "compact objects" candidates, some of them could be dwarf elliptical galaxies.

- Fahrion et al. 2019
  - TItle: Single metal-poor ultra compact dwarf galaxy at one kiloparsec distance from the low-mass elliptical galaxy FCC 47
  - ADS: https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2019A%26A...625A..50F/abstract
  - CDS: https://vizier.cds.unistra.fr/viz-bin/VizieR-3?-source=J/A%2bA/625/A50
  - Number of UCD candidates: 381
  - Notes: this catalog is for "UCDs in the literature", which includes 13 different papers, some of them overlap with the saved database (Liu+2015; Misgeld+2011). So a redundancy check is necessary.

## Detailed Source Information

- Saifollahi et al. (2021)
  - MNRAS 504, 3580
  - Title: Ultra-compact dwarfs beyond the centre of the Fornax galaxy cluster
  - Region: Fornax cluster
  - Contribution: 604 UCDs (largest single source)
  - Notes: Extended Fornax sample with half-light radius measurements

- Voggel et al. (2020)
  - ApJ 899, 140 ⭐ KEY PAPER
  - Title: The Nature of Ultra-compact Dwarfs Revealed by Gaia
  - Region: Centaurus group (NGC 5128 / Cen A)
  - Contribution: 689 UCD candidates
  - Method: Gaia-based selection using AEN, color, and proper motion
  - Notes: This is the methodology paper that inspired our search pipeline

- Zhang et al. (2015)
  - ApJ 802, 30
  - Title: NGVS VI. The Kinematics of Ultra-compact Dwarfs in M87
  - Region: Virgo cluster (M87)
  - Contribution: Spectroscopically confirmed UCDs
  - Notes: Important for validation — known Virgo UCDs

- Liu et al. (2015)
  - ApJ 812, 34
  - Title: NGVS X. Properties of Ultra-compact Dwarfs in the M87, M49, and M60 Regions
  - Region: Virgo cluster (M87, M49, M60)
  - Contribution: 105 UCD candidates from NGVS imaging

- Misgeld et al. (2011)
  - A&A 531, A4 (1/2)
  - Title: A large population of ultra-compact dwarf galaxies in the Hydra I cluster
  - Region: Hydra I cluster
  - Contribution: 118 UCDs

- Chiboucas et al. (2011)
  - ApJ 737, 86
  - Title: Near-infrared Properties of Ultra-compact Dwarfs in the Coma Cluster
  - Region: Coma cluster
  - Contribution: 26 UCDs with NIR photometry
